Lifestyle objects created using modern prototyping techniques and handcrafted by local artisans in Barcelona
Los Objetos Decorativos aims to create a collection of lifestyle elements that are present on the daily scene.
The essence of the project is to keep on creating elements through the exploration of new materials and contemporary techniques.
